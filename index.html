What is You Got This From Home?

Let's talk more about the skills that require emotional labor.

You Got This is a network of community conferences on the non-technical 'core' skills needed for a happy, healthy work life. You Got This From Home is a new, free, online edition of the event taking place on June 6 2020.

FAQ
What are you doing to make this event accessible?
The event will be live captioned and will be included in the stream. Post-event we will provide recordings and transcripts. Speakers are paid for their time.
Can I submit a talk?
Not this time. We are reaching out to folks who submitted excellent talks to our previous physical events but did not get chosen in the review process. We will still run a talk submission process at our physical events.
What are the social sessions?
We're still working it out, but we feel it's important to try and provide social networking opportunities as well as broadcast talks. Hang tight for info.
How much does it cost to sponsor?
